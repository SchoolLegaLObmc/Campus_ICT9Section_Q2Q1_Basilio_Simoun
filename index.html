The Big 3

The Future and Gamble of Online banking

Bitcoin

Bitcoin, the original cryptocurrency, stands out due to its decentralized nature, secure blockchain technology, and limited supply, making it a valuable digital asset with potential for long-term growth.

Ethereum

Ethereum stands out as a pioneering platform for smart contracts, enabling developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) without intermediaries. This programmable blockchain offers a versatile ecosystem for various innovative projects, from decentralized finance (DeFi) to non-fungible tokens (NFTs).

BNB

BNB, the native cryptocurrency of the Binance Smart Chain, offers high-speed and low-cost transactions. It's used for fees, staking, and accessing various services within the Binance ecosystem
